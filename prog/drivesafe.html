PROBLEM 1

There are 260 commercial vehicle fatalities in Australia every year, 21% of all road deaths

PROBLEM 2

IVMS safety reports take a month or more to be delivered to a driver’s manager. Drivers never recieve this feedback

Solution

A platform for instantaneous feedback on safety & performance and seamless integration of a driver’s workflow

Prototype Demo

Recap

  • Reduces the number of vehicle incidents
  • Instantaneous feedback for drivers & managers
  • Easy and intuitive to use web app
  • Seamless integration of driver workflow
