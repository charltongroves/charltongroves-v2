How

This website was written from scratch using HTML5, CSS, and JS. Everything was written without a framework/template aside from the navbar which is from bootstrap

Why

This website started as a side project around august 2016, I had just finished a web design course and wanted to put my skills to the test.
It started out not that great.
To get an idea of what it looked like back then, go to charltongroves.com/crap.html.

This websites primary goal was and still is to be a kind of digital hub of things i've made. It's goal has evolved since then to be more of a digital resume.

Richard Groves Architects

november 2016 - present

How

richardgrovesarchitects.com was written from scratch using HTML5, CSS, and JS(jquery). The navbar was from bootstrap

Why

My dad needed a website made for his architecture firm. His old website was extremely outdated and he has absolutely no digital presence
being the good son that I am, I made him one. I worked closely with him throughout the entire design process, adhearing to his every requirment. And drew many many paper prototypes until a design he liked was found.

The website took over 40 hours to make (most of which was redesigning parts he didn't like). I learnt a lot throughout the design process, primarily, how important the requirements/prototyping portion of software development. One miscommunication during that phase can lead to a lot of clean up down the road...
